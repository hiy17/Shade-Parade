About Shade Parade

Shade Parade is a web app designed to help designers, artists, and developers manage and organize color palettes with ease. It allows users to input, sort, and save their color combinations based on various attributes like lightness or hue using different sorting algorithms. Discover the perfect shades for your projects and streamline your design workflow!

How to Use

  • Use the Color Picker or enter hex codes directly to set up your colors.
  • Choose a sorting algorithm (Bubble, Quick, or Merge) and sort by:
    • Lightness: Sorts colors from dark to light.
    • Hue: Sorts colors according to the ROYGBIV (Red, Orange, Yellow, Green, Blue, Indigo, Violet) spectrum.
  • Save your sorted palettes by clicking the "Save Palette" button.
  • Access saved palettes and your sorting history via the menu (☰ button).
  • Download or clear palettes as needed for further use in your projects.
